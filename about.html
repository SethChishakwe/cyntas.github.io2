Investing Made Simple

If you've never invested before, the whole process might seem confusing. Let us break it down into simple, manageable steps that anyone can understand.

Think of it like farming...

You start with seeds (your money), plant them in good soil (investment opportunities), and with time and care, they grow into a bigger harvest (returns). At Cyntas, we help you choose the right seeds and soil for your situation.

Common Questions

Is my money safe?

Yes. We use secure trust accounts, provide regular audits, and maintain full transparency about where every dollar is invested. We're registered and comply with Zimbabwe financial regulations.

Can I start with just $10?

Absolutely! Our micro-investing model is designed specifically for people starting small. Many of our most successful investors began with just $10-20 per month.

What if I need my money back?

Most investments have fixed terms (6-12 months), but we offer flexible options. We recommend keeping an emergency fund separate from investments.

How are returns paid?

Returns are paid directly to your preferred method - EcoCash, bank account, or cash collection. You'll receive clear statements showing exactly what you've earned.
